General Command

/Dice [Number], /Roll [Number]

Rolls the dice within a set range. Default is 100. You can choose a number between 1 and 1000. Only Group, Alliance, or nearby characters can see the results.

Ex) /dice 100

/GroupSplit [Amount]

Distributes the entered amount equally to all group members.

Ex) /groupsplit 1000

/ForceSplit [Amount]

Distributes the entered amount equally to all Alliance members.

Ex) /forcesplit 23000

/Free

Group members obtain looted items freely.

/RoundRobinLoot, /InOrder

Group members obtain looted items in a round-robin manner.

/GroupLeaderLoot, /Captain

The group leader loots all of the items.

/RecruitGroupMember [Introduction]

Creates a <Recruit Group> link in the chat window. When a user right-clicks this link, a link containing the level and class information of the user is sent to you as a Whisper, which you can then us to decline or invite the user to your group. Users can also click the <Recruit Group> link to view the group's information.

Ex) /RecruitGroupMember Looking for a Lv 50 Sorcerer willing to go to Dark Poeta.

/ApplyForGroup [Introduction]

Creates an <Apply For Group> link in the chat window. When the group leader right-clicks this link, an invitation to the group gets sent automatically. A user can click the <Apply For Group> link and view the author's information.

Ex) /ApplyForGroup Lv 50 Assassin looking for a Dredgion group.

/InvitetoGroup [CharacterName], /invite [CharacterName]

Invites a character to the group.

Ex) /invite Jarumonerk (Group Leader only)

/Leavegroup, /Leave

Leaves the group you have joined.

/Kick CharacterName

Removes a group member from the group. (Group leaders only)

Ex) /Kick Shuma

/Brand 1-14

Displays a sign above the head of the selected target. (Group or Alliance leaders only)

Ex) /Sign 6

/SelectBrand 1-14

Selects the target on which the sign is displayed.

Ex) /selectbrand 6

/Alert [Message]

Changes to Alert chat mode. Sends the message to the chat window and the HUD of all group members. (Group leaders only)

Ex) /alert Everyone get ready to charge!

/Group, /G

Changes to group chat mode. Only your group receives group chat.

/GG [Message]

Sends a message to your group without changing chat modes.

/Start Mentoring

Begins mentoring a group member who is at least 10 levels lower than you.

Mentors do not get XP, Kinah, or common items, but the mentored Daeva will get the normal amount of these things. There is no penalty for playing with a mentor like there is for playing with a non-mentoring higher-level character.

/End Mentoring

Stops mentoring.

/Recruit Mentor [your text here]

Type either of these commands, followed by a space, and then your personalized message, to create a <Recruit Mentor Group> link in the chat window. Other users can click the link to view information about your group and apply for it. Note that copy/pasting the slash command will not work.

The applicant's level and class information are sent to you as a Whisper, and you can then choose to invite or decline the player. <Recruit Mentor Group> posts are displayed in the Find Group window regardless of level.

ex) /Recruit Mentor Please help me with a difficult quest.

/Apply As Mentor [your text here]

Type either of these commands, followed by a space, and then your personalized message, to create an <Apply as Mentor Group> link in the chat window. Other users can click the link to view poster information and apply for the group. Note that copy/pasting the slash command will not work.

<Apply as Mentor Group> posts are displayed in the Find Group window regardless of level.

ex) /Apply as Mentor I'm available to help with difficult quests.

Private Channel Command

When you press the <Enter> key to open the Chat window and then click the Speech Bubble icon, it opens a pop-up menu. You can see private channel commands when you select <Manage Private Chat>.

/ChannelNotification [Number] [Message]

The chat leader sends an alert message to all members.

Ex) /ChannelNotification 4 Everyone get ready to charge!
